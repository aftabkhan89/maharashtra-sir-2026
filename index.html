🗳️ Maharashtra SIR 2026 Timeline

Special Intensive Revision of Electoral Rolls (Phase 3)
📅 Announced by ECI on 14 May 2026

What is SIR and why does it matter?

SIR is a special voter-roll verification exercise. BLOs will visit households and verify existing voters through pre-filled Enumeration Forms. Your details may be checked against the earlier electoral roll and family linkage records. If your record cannot be linked clearly, you may need to provide additional verification. This timeline explains what happens, when it happens, and what you should do.

⏱️ Most important deadline coming up BLO visit window closes on 29 July 2026

Key deadline
1
Qualifying Date
1 October 2026
The reference date. Anyone who will be 18 or older on this date is eligible to be on the voter roll. Think of it as a snapshot moment used to decide who counts as a voter.
📌 No action needed. Just the rulebook date.
2
Training & Printing
20 to 29 June 2026
BLOs (Booth Level Officers) get trained. Pre-filled enumeration forms are printed for every existing voter. This is behind-the-scenes work. Nothing visible to you yet.
⚙️ Government prep phase. Sit tight.
3
BLO Door-to-Door Visit ⚠️ Critical
30 June to 29 July 2026
BLOs visit households with pre-filled Enumeration Forms. Check your details carefully, sign where required, and return the form. This is the most important verification window, so keep your current EPIC, Aadhaar, address proof, and old voter records ready if available.
🚨 Be available. Verify details carefully. Keep documents ready.
4
Draft Electoral Roll Published
5 August 2026
The first version of the new voter list comes out. Go check if your name is on it. If it's there with correct details, great. If not, you have one month to file an objection.
🔍 Check your name on voters.eci.gov.in
5
Claims & Objections Window
5 Aug to 4 Sept 2026
One-month window to fix errors. Wrong details? File Form 8. Name missing? File Form 6. Duplicate entry? Check with BLO/ERO and use Form 7 if deletion is required. This is your safety net if the BLO visit went wrong.
📝 File correction forms if needed
6
Hearings & Disposal
5 Aug to 3 Oct 2026
ERO (Electoral Registration Officer) reviews objections, holds hearings if needed, and decides each case. If you filed something in Step 5, this is when it gets processed.
⚖️ Attend hearing if called
7
Final Electoral Roll Published 🎉
7 October 2026
The final voter list is published. Check your name again. If your name is missing or wrongly changed, follow the appeal or correction route given by election authorities.
✅ Final check. Save proof of your voter record

📋 Quick Action Plan

  • Now till end of June: Keep all documents ready (Aadhaar, current EPIC, old voter records, family proofs). Resolve any pending issues like duplicate entries.
  • 30 June to 29 July: When BLO visits, hand over signed EF for every family member. This is the make-or-break window.
  • 5 August: Check the draft roll. Make sure all family names appear correctly.
  • Before 4 September: File any corrections through Form 6, 7, or 8 on voters.eci.gov.in.
  • 7 October: Verify on the final roll. Your name should be there, mapped to your current address.
  • Always: Verify your own record on voters.eci.gov.in or through the Voter Helpline app before filing any form.
Source: Election Commission of India Press Note ECI/PN/086/2026 dated 14 May 2026. Maharashtra schedule under SIR Phase III.
